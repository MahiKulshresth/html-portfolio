Best 5 Series according to MAHI

My top 5 Series of all time

1. Vampire Diaries

It is about Vampires, filled with fiction, action, romance, mystery, thriller and fantasy.

2. Brooklyn 99

It is a light comic series revolving around the daily life of a group of detectives at 99th
precint at Brooklyn.

3. Bridgerton

It is a beautiful romantic series set in the 18th century London.

4. Stranger Things

It is one of the best sci-fi series. It involves a group of friends
dealing with the troubles coming from parallel dimension call the "Upside Down"

5. Dark

It is literally one of the most "dark" series ever. Totally mind bending. Too Good
for someone who loves deep and complex science fiction.
